Kicks






Basics

There are a bunch of kicks you can learn! You can learn basics, all the way up to some of the most hard kicks. Some of the basics are, Front Kicks, Round Kicks, and Side Kicks! Some of the harder ones are, Jump Outer Crescent Kicks, Butterfly Kicks, and Reverse Hook Kicks.

If you're a beginner, those all may sound hard! The truth is, they aren't. With a little practice you can learn your first kick in a week! Maybe even a day! They won't be perfect in that amount of time, but your knowledge on how to do it will definitely be there. So with out further a do, let's learn your first kick!
